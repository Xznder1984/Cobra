## Design Elements

### Snake Head Geometry (for Canva shapes)
- Use overlapping rounded triangles for the hood
- Head: elongated diamond/oval
- Eyes: two small circles, gold fill, black slit pupils
- Scales: subtle V-shaped patterns on the hood (dark teal)
- Tongue: thin forked line in coral

### Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold, or Inter Bold
- **Body**: Inter, Source Sans Pro, or system sans-serif
- **Code**: JetBrains Mono, Fira Code, or Cascadia Code
- **Logo font**: Custom or Montserrat Black with modified 'O'
